Shreen Harmony is a community choir based in Mere, Wiltshire. Under the musical directorship of Caroline Radcliffe, the choir meet on a Thursday during term time, usually at the United Reform Church in Boar Street, Mere and perform throughout the year in the local area at different events, ranging from church fetes to more established organised gatherings, like fundraising concerts for local churches, the annual fundraising concert in aid of the Alzheimerâ€™s Society and a Christmas Concert in aid of a different charity each year.

The choir sing a variety of musical genres from all around the world, including traditional British, African, Eastern European, Spiritual and American folk, fun being at the heart of it all. There is a wonderfully informal attitude in the group and there is no audition. Please contact Judy Fray on 01747 840913 for further information.
